Cancellation and No Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Course Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your course registration, however:

  • All course registrations: No refunds available
  • Payment processing: All sales are final
  • Course access: Immediate upon payment confirmation

2. No Refund Policy

MindScript operates under a strict no refund policy:

  • All payments are final and non-refundable
  • No refunds will be processed under any circumstances
  • Course fees are non-transferable

Important: All sales are final. No refunds will be processed regardless of the payment method used during registration.

3. How to Request Cancellation

To cancel your course registration (without refund):

  1. Send an email to: support@mindscript.com
  2. Include your registration ID and reason for cancellation
  3. Provide the payment transaction ID from Razorpay
  4. Wait for confirmation email (within 24 hours)

4. Non-Refundable Items

All items and services are non-refundable including:

  • Course materials and content
  • Live sessions and recordings
  • Administrative fees
  • Transaction processing fees
  • Course completion certificates

5. Special Circumstances

Even in special circumstances, no refunds will be provided:

  • Medical emergencies
  • Technical issues
  • Course cancellation by MindScript (alternative arrangements will be made)
  • Personal emergencies

6. Course Transfer

Instead of cancellation, you may request to:

  • Transfer to a different course (subject to price difference)
  • Defer to a future batch
  • Transfer to another person (with additional processing fees)

Transfer requests are subject to availability and may incur additional fees.
