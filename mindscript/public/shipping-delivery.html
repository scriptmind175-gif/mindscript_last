Shipping and Delivery Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Digital Course Delivery

MindScript courses are delivered digitally through our online learning platform. No physical shipping is required.

2. Course Access Timeline

Upon successful payment through Razorpay:

  • Immediate Access: Course materials and pre-recorded content
  • Within 24 hours: Welcome email with login credentials
  • Within 48 hours: Access to live session schedule
  • Within 72 hours: Mentor assignment and introduction

Note: Course access is granted immediately after payment confirmation from Razorpay.

3. Delivery Method

Course delivery includes:

  • Secure login credentials sent via email
  • Access to our Learning Management System (LMS)
  • Downloadable course materials and resources
  • Live session links and meeting details
  • Mobile app access for on-the-go learning

4. System Requirements

To access your course, you need:

  • Stable internet connection (minimum 2 Mbps)
  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Computer, tablet, or smartphone
  • Audio and video capabilities for live sessions

5. Delivery Confirmation

You will receive delivery confirmation through:

  • Email confirmation from MindScript
  • SMS notification (if phone number provided)
  • WhatsApp message (if opted in)
  • Dashboard notification in your LMS account

6. Troubleshooting Access Issues

If you experience access issues:

  1. Check your email (including spam folder)
  2. Verify your internet connection
  3. Clear browser cache and cookies
  4. Contact support at: support@mindscript.com

7. Course Material Updates

Course materials are regularly updated:

  • New content added monthly
  • Real-time industry updates
  • Additional practice exercises
  • Updated project requirements
